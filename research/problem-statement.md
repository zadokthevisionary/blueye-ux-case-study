# Problem Statement

Modern professionals rely heavily on digital devices such as laptops, tablets, and smartphones to complete daily work tasks. As digital workflows continue to expand across industries, individuals are spending prolonged hours interacting with screens. This extended screen exposure has been associated with a range of negative side effects including eye strain, headaches, mental fatigue, disrupted sleep patterns, and reduced overall well-being.

Although awareness of these issues has increased, digital device usage remains unavoidable in many professional environments. Work productivity, communication, and information access are deeply integrated with digital tools, making it unrealistic to eliminate or significantly reduce screen usage altogether.

Because of this reality, many existing solutions focus on limiting screen time, which can conflict with the productivity demands of modern work. This creates a gap in the market for solutions that acknowledge the necessity of digital devices while helping users reduce the negative physiological and mental side effects of prolonged screen exposure.

This challenge presents an opportunity to design a digital wellness tool that focuses on harm reduction rather than restriction, enabling professionals to continue working efficiently while improving their overall digital health.

# Proposed Solution
BLUEYE – A Blue Light Mode Application for Harm Reduction

The proposed solution is BLUEYE, a digital wellness application designed to reduce the harmful effects associated with prolonged screen exposure.

The concept was inspired by blue light filtering glasses, which are commonly marketed as a method to reduce eye strain caused by digital screens. While these glasses can provide benefits, they require users to purchase and consistently wear an additional physical accessory. This introduces barriers such as inconvenience, cost, and personal preference, as not all users are comfortable wearing glasses while working.

BLUEYE translates this concept into a software-based solution directly integrated into digital devices. Instead of requiring an external product, the application provides built-in protective features that can be accessed through a user's device.

The application focuses on several key functions:

1. Screen Time Awareness

BLUEYE provides users with insights into how much time they spend on digital devices through daily, weekly, and monthly screen time reports. Increasing awareness allows users to better understand their habits and identify periods of excessive screen usage.

2. Automated Eye Protection

Users can enable Auto Protection Mode, which automatically activates blue light filtering when screen exposure exceeds a predefined limit. This reduces eye strain during long work sessions without requiring constant manual adjustments.

3. Goal-Based Screen Health Management

Users can set personalized screen time goals and allow the application to support healthier device usage patterns over time.

4. Passive Harm Reduction

Rather than restricting device use, BLUEYE operates in the background to reduce the intensity of screen exposure, allowing professionals to maintain productivity while minimizing health risks.

# Design Philosophy

The core philosophy behind BLUEYE is harm reduction through automation and awareness. Instead of forcing users to reduce screen usage — which may not be feasible in many professional environments — the application focuses on mitigating the negative impacts of prolonged device interaction.

By combining screen time awareness, automated protective features, and goal-based wellness tracking, BLUEYE aims to support professionals in maintaining healthier relationships with their digital devices.
