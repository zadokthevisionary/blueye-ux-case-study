# User Interview Questions

Can you walk me through your daily digital usage journey? (Apps used, business/personal tasks, etc)

In this last week, about how much screen time did you use?

Please describe to me how you feel after you use your device for an extended amount of time? 

Do you feel your digital usage is necessary for your business and personal goals?

When you are overwhelmed with tasks, on a scale from 1-5 how much do you depend on your device to assist you with them? 

Have you ever experienced any side effects of excessive screen time?

Explain to me a time you had a bad or good experience with a health focused digital tool, if any, and why?
