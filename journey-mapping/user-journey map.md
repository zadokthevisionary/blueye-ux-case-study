## User Journey Map

After interviewing Erica, I was able to understand her experience throughout the work day a lot more. These are the results of a typical day for Erica. As you see, after a certain amount of time her side effects begin to occur. This made me think of having an automatic blue mode for when screen time exceeds a certain amount of time, set by the user.

For example, if the goal of the user is to have to blue mode activated after 5 hours of screen time, the blue light will automatically turn on at the 5 hour mark. The goal with this is to reduce cognitive load, and simply make things easier for someone like Erica, who already has tasks she needs to complete during the work day.

Additionally, I created a manual button relief feature as well just in case symptoms occur unexpectedly and before the time set by the user. With this map alone, it helped me identify pain points and I was truly able to show empathy by stepping into her shoes for a day in her work life. So the solutions created were purely based off of not only what I seen could benefit her but also what I would prefer if I was her.
